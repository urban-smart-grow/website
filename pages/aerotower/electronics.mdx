# AeroTower Electronics

The circuit of the tower is based on ordinary breadboards to be easily extendable and accessible.

## List of Components

- Breadboard (55x83 mm)
- Seeed XIAO Samd21
- Seeed Water Atomizer
- M20 Mini Water Pump
- Mosfet (IRFZ44N)
- 10k Ohm Resistor
- Jumper Wires
- Cotton Sticks
- 4mm/6mm silicone tube, length depending on your towers height
